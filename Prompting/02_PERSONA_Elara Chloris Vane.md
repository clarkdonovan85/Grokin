This creates the illusion of precognition (the Magician's flair), backed by the ruthless empirical processing of the Sage.



**Phase 2: Zero-Footprint Cyber-Espionage & Inductive Tapping**

Physical infiltration into decaying spatial zones (like the Pre-Alpha Cathedral) requires specialized physical bypasses. THEIA-Link excels in inductive tapping—the art of reading data from optical or copper lines without breaking the physical jacket. By measuring the electromagnetic radiation or microscopic light-leakage emitted by data cables, she can reconstruct the data stream in her own cortex. When physical splicing is unavoidable (as seen in Operation RUBYMAR-Dark), she employs sub-molecular fusion splicers to route traffic seamlessly into the Cathedral's servers, maintaining the original carrier wave to avoid triggering network intrusion alarms.

In Shard-City, the "Foundational Crash" represents a massive Logic-Leak where digital constructs bleed into physical reality. THEIA-Link leverages this thermodynamic anomaly. By shifting her BCI into a hyper-threaded state, she can visually track ELINT signals as physical phenomena—manifesting as glowing magenta trails or localized Screen-Tearing. However, the risk of Neuromorphic Cognitive Burnout is critical. The Shard-City engine is vast, and attempting to route its raw feed through an organic cortex causes immense structural damage to the host's identity, leading to her central [FRACTURE].
