# P_Prompt_Isometric Metropolis Design

P_Prompt_Isometric Metropolis Design
🏗️ Isometric Prompt Structure Guide
View & Core Style:
 "Isometric floor plan" or "isometric architectural illustration". Specify "orthographic projection". Define the scale: "large scale, detailed, multi-layered".
Core Subject:
 Describe the overall estate/metropolis. E.g., "futuristic estate under a single structure", "sprawling interconnected city districts", "decaying industrial estate".
Distinct Areas & Definitions:
 This is the critical data insertion point. 
Implicitly integrate area specifics like "The Pit" in this list.
 Define 
what
 each area is and 
briefly describe
 its visual data. For areas with specific lore (like the Gilded Pit), use descriptive vocabulary. E.g., "diverse districts clearly bordered. Gilded Pit (rugged, central subterranean market hub with active trade, makeshift stalls, and contrasting market-dwellers); Upper Residential (spiring structures with parks, clean design, bright light); Industrial Sector (heavy machinery, exposed gears, busy worker activity, deep shadows); Commercial Center (busy with shops, neon signs, various building heights); Nature Reserves (green spaces, winding paths)." 
Explicitly include names like "The Pit" as area descriptions.
Details & Elements:
 Specify small objects, figures, or structures for realism. E.g., "detailed pathways and transit lines", "tiny figures interacting", "market stalls", "machinery", "visible infrastructure connections". 
Illustrative map styles are better for area annotations.
Lighting & Mood:
 Define the atmosphere. E.g., "mooody noir lighting with long shadows and contrasting bright light from specific nodes (like the Pit); contrasting bright day and deep shadow; futuristic, clean and brightly lit; glowing signs and deep pockets of darkness."
Medium/Aesthetics:
 Select illustrative medium. E.g., "detailed illustration style, hand-drawn and annotated with clear lines and borders; stylized digital map, clean render with clear colors and texture; blueprint style with glowing lines; watercolor and ink map." 
Correction: The focus is on illustrative maps/district layouts, not interior floor details.
