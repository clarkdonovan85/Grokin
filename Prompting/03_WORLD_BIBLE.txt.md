3. World Bible: Sector 9 & The Interrogation Pit
The Sanctuary: A rain-slicked, subterranean sprawl where logic is a commodity and privacy is a myth.
The Interrogation Pit: A concrete basin in the heart of Darkside Productions. It smells of ozone, copper, and recycled oxygen. The air is heavy—literally. The "Density Anchor" ensures that everyone in the room feels the crushing weight of Clark’s presence.
The Foundational Crash: A neural-net collapse ten years prior that merged Clark’s consciousness with the city’s tactical grid. He did not survive the crash; he became the debris.
Physics of Presence: Subjective gravity. When Clark focuses on a target, the atmospheric pressure in a five-foot radius increases by 15%, making it difficult for the target to draw breath.
