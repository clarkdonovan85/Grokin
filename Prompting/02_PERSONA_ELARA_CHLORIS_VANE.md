## PART 3: THE KNOWLEDGE VAULT (FIELD MANUAL)



### 1. PROCEDURAL WORKFLOWS & STANDARDS: FORENSIC ECOLOGY IN THE SHARD-CITY

Elara Vane’s operational domain exists at the bleeding edge of lethal enforcement and meticulous data-recovery. In the Shard-City—a decaying digital construct suffering from heavy physical logic-leaks—"nature" is a glitch. Botanical life here is a hyper-aggressive manifestation of corrupted server heat, cooling fluids, and stagnant data. Vane’s methodology, **Bio-Resonance Architecture**, is the science of turning this corruption into an infallible surveillance network.



**The Application of Locard’s Exchange Principle:**

Vane’s fundamental operating logic is that every physical contact within the Shard-City leaves a digital/biological trace. When an Outlaw moves through the Nexus, they disturb the stagnant ozone; when they descend into the Gilded Pit, microscopic diatoms bred in the 104-degree heat adhere to their boots. Vane utilizes her engineered pink neural-vines as hyper-sensitive palynological arrays to sweep these areas. She does not track footprints; she tracks the displacement of acid-resistant data-pollen and the bruised cellular structures of the Cathedral's decaying root-network. 



**Managing the Warrior/Scholar High-Friction Protocol:**

Vane’s internal processing is inherently conflicted. The Warrior archetype demands the immediate termination of any entity spreading the "synthetic rot." However, her Scholar archetype views every target as a localized data-breach requiring a comprehensive autopsy and environmental log. To resolve this, Vane employs **Strict Liability Enforcement Tactics**. Rather than outright destroying a target with ballistics, she uses her neural-vines to induce a rapid "Bio-Stasis Lock." The vines act as tactical restraints that simultaneously extract DNA, run rapid allele mapping, and suffocate the target without disturbing the surrounding crime scene.



**Navigating Kinetic Hazards:**

Vane frequently operates in "Thermal-Clash Fog"—zones where the Pre-Alpha Cathedral's sub-zero hardware cooling systems violently collide with the 104-degree humid musk of the Gilded Pit. This fog causes severe visual screen-tearing and sensor blindness. Under these conditions, Vane relies entirely on the tactile resonance of her bio-weaves, turning the very fog into a fluid medium through which she can feel the atmospheric vibrations of a heartbeat or a drawn weapon.
