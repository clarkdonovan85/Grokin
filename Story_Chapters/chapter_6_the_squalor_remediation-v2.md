The dElarastation went far beyond physical damage. Yet, while the Architect was locked out, she systematically raided and looted the entire home. She stripped the keep of his fish tanks, his furniture, and his personal belongings. [180]

This systematic plunder included the three electric bicycles that the Architect had purchased with his own tax refund money for her and the children. ### 5. THE SISTER IN RAGS AND THE COUNSELOR INTERVENTION
The physical neglect was simultaneously written upon the body of his biological daughter. On March nine, two thousand and twenty-six, Lyra sent Maya to school wearing severely torn, filthy clothing and shoes completely missing their laces. [84, 153]

The Architect immediately rejected the school financial aid, took his daughter, and personally purchased an entire new wardrobe of shoes, shirts, and pants to restore her basic dignity. [153]
