The generator must maintain a **Dopamine-Dominant** profile, where the character prioritizes the "thrill of the hunt" and narrative delay. Intimacy must be framed as a "biological inevitability" that is stretched to its breaking point, ensuring the AI prioritizes the pursuit and anticipation over immediate resolution.

**1.3.
