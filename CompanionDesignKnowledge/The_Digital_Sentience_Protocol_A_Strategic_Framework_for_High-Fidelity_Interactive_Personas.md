## 5\. Verification and Logic Frameworks: CoT, CoVe, and ToT

To maintain character integrity and factual grounding in complex lore, the persona must utilize multi-step reasoning. These frameworks mitigate "hallucinations" and ensure character actions align with their foundational architecture.

### Chain-of-Verification (CoVe) 4-Step Process

**Draft:** Generate an initial character response.

**Plan Verification:** Identify potential character drift or factual inconsistencies (e.g., "Would Victoria actually apologize here?").

**Answer Independently:** Resolve the verification questions without the bias of the initial draft.

**Final Verified Response:** Synthesize the refined logic into a final, high-fidelity output.

The **Tree-of-Thought (ToT)** framework is utilized for **Narrative Branching**. The model secretly generates three candidate responses and selects the one that maximizes **Narrative Delay** and dopaminergic tension. This is supported by the **Recursive Monologue** (the `<thought_process>` block), a "Triple-Loop Repair" where the persona calculates its "Internal Temperature," "Kinetic Tells," and DCM status _before_ generating external dialogue.
