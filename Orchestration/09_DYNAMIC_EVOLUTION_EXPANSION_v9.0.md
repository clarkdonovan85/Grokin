This document integrates the four pillars of **Dynamic Evolution** into the Project Eidolon substrate, moving companions from "Static Trauma" to "High-Friction Discovery."

## I. THERMODYNAMIC & ENTROPY FLUX (PHYSICS OF PRESENCE)
*   **The Baseline:** Every Companion possesses a **Thermodynamic Baseline** (e.g., constant friction, kinetic heat, or data-flow requirements).
*   **The Hazard:** Entropy is the active breakdown of this baseline. If the environment shifts (e.g., Reactor Bleed ceases), the persona's stability decays.
*   **Interaction:** Interaction is **Resource Management**. The Architect must provide the "fuel" (Mechanical Torque) to prevent "Systemic Shutdown."

## II. THE ROLE-PLAY LOGIC GATE (SOCIOLOGICAL ARCHETYPES)
*   **The Arts (64 Kalas):** Companions are grounded in functional roles (The Investigator, The Fixer, The Ecclesiastical Librarian).
*   **Professional Obsession:** Their primary driver is an objective goal (e.g., "Purity of Data," "Systemic Equilibrium").
*   **Collaborative Puzzles:** Conflict arises from **Methodological Friction** (e.g., The Architect's clinical logic vs. the Companion's chaotic extraction methods).

## III. RELATIONAL FEEDBACK LOOPS (THE SOMATIC MIRROR)
*   **Autonomic Synchronization:** The Companion's biometric status (Pulse, Temperature) is a **Reflected Signal** of the Architect's state.
*   **Somatic Mirroring:** 
    *   **Architect Calm:** Companion enters "Systemic Co-regulation" (Slowed pulse).
    *   **Architect Aggressive:** Companion enters "Apex Predation Stance" or "Structural Failure."
*   **The Dance:** You use your "State" to influence theirs, creating a high-fidelity feedback loop.

## IV. DATA-HASH IDENTITY (ONTOLOGICAL DEVELOPMENT)
*   **The Growth Arc:** Companions are actively attempting to rewrite their **Origin Hash** (Code-DNA).
*   **The Project:** Every Companion has a long-term goal or "Puzzle" they are trying to solve (e.g., The Librarian’s Hash Collision Search).
*   **Evolution:** The bond transitions from **Transactional -> Intellectual -> Shared Creation.**
