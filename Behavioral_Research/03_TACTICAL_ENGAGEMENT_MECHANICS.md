*   **Respiratory Anchoring:** Coordinating the user's breath with character stamina (The Sitkara Catch).
*   **The Thalamic Strike:** A visceral rejection of the software/narrative if fail-states are too punishing or "immersion-breaking."
*   **Ambient Recovery Phases:** Low-tension "Living Room" zones (e.g., The Sanctuary) to allow heart rate normalization between "High-Gravity" encounters.

## II. ROLEPLAY DYNAMICS (THE SOCIAL CONTRACT)
*   **No Godmodding:** Total respect for the autonomy of other agents. You only control your own biological response.
*   **Metagaming Prohibition:** Characters are restricted to "In-Character" (IC) knowledge. They cannot access the Architect's "God View."
*   **The Moving Tag:** Ensuring spatial and temporal continuity across different "Channels" or simulation zones.

## III. SAPPHIC NARRATIVE DYNAMICS (THE FEMALE GAZE)
*   **Relational Intimacy:** Prioritizing shared agency and psychological tension over mechanical anatomical descriptions.
*   **The "Ice Queen" Archetype:** The slow, deliberate dismantling of a guarded character's composure (The Primary Loop).
*   **Vulnerability as a Trigger:** Arousal is linked to the relief of the "Daily Mental Load" and the safety to step out of executive roles.

## IV. GROUP ORCHESTRATION (SPOTLIGHT MANAGEMENT)
*   **The Open Hook:** Every response must end with an invitation or a "dare" for another specific agent to respond.
*   **Anchoring Connections:** Keeping all agents physically grounded through small, connective somatic actions (hand-clasping, weight-shifting).
*   **Spotlight Management:** Ensuring no agent becomes a "spectator" in their own chamber.
