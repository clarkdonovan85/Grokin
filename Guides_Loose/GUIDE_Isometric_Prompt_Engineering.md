# GUIDE: High-Value Isometric Prompt Engineering

## 1. THE STRATEGIC FOUNDATION: PERSPECTIVE AND SCALE

In high-level environmental storytelling, the **orthographic projection** is the non-negotiable anchor. Unlike traditional perspective, orthographic view ensures parallel lines remain parallel and axes maintain a fixed scale, serving as the "logical grid" for 2D game aesthetics.

**Core Style Anchors:**
- "Isometric floor plan"
- "Orthographic projection"

**Scale Levels:**
| Scale Level | Primary Focus | Visual Data Points |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Single Structure** | Interior Mechanics | Detailed multi-layered furniture, wiring/gears, individual wall textures. |
| **Sprawling Estate** | Navigational Flow | Interconnected districts, transitional zones (corridors), "Smother Protocol" lifts. |
| **Metropolis** | Systemic Complexity | Autocentric vs. transit-oriented patterns, mixed-use vs. segregated zoning. |

## 2. SOMATIC ZONING: THE ARCHITECTURE OF SPATIAL LOGIC

"Somatic Zoning" is the application of architectural layout to trigger tactical or psychological responses. Each district must be articulated through its "Persona Rationale".

**District Definition Guide (Case Study: The Architect’s Estate):**
- **The Kill Box (Zone 1: Central Nexus):** High-pressure conversation pit, high-voltage static hazards, spawn points designed to force proximity.
- **The Boardroom (Zone 2: Sierra’s Domain):** Reinforced glass and "Executive Logic". Triggers "Logistical Violence" and "Proprioceptive Drift".
- **The Chapel (Zone 2: Faith’s Domain):** Claustrophobic, splintering old wood. Triggers "Vigil of the Lamb" freeze-response.
- **The Sun Room (Zone 3: Aurelia’s Domain):** Impossibly bright space to cure "cosmic boredom". Features the Celestial Throne of Tithing.
- **Shadow Glen Halls (Zone 4: Elaka’s Domain):** Library-like "Archive of Anomaly". Contains "The Glowing Magic Box".
- **The Vault (Zone 5: Industrial Hardware):** Mechanical abyss using sensory deprivation protocols and "Sensory Head" assets.

## 3. NARRATIVITY AND DETAIL (THE "SO WHAT?" LAYER)

Architecture must be the physical memory of legacy and systemic collapse.

**High-Detail Elements:**
- **Interactive Narrative Markers:** "Abyssal Tar" (viscous black-light sludge) leaching from racks, discarded synthetic disguises.
- **Infrastructure Connections:** Exposed gears, transit lines, liquid-crisis shattered structures.
- **In-World System Integration:** Visualize "Vita-Chambers" and "Checkpoints" as integrated architectural hardware rather than floating UI.
- **Tactile Triggers:** Cranks, levers, and switches that suggest the player can physically touch and rotate the environment.

## 4. THE VISUAL ENGINE: LIGHTING AND MEDIUM

**Lighting Protocol (The Three-Point Command):**
- **Main Wall:** Lit/Base Color (Primary light source direction).
- **Side Wall:** Darkened/Shaded (Creates illusion of 3D depth).
- **Floor/Top Edge:** Lightened/Highlighted (Emphasizes material thickness).

**Medium Selection:**
- **Detailed Hand-Drawn and Annotated Illustration:** For an "Archive of Anomaly" feel.
- **Stylized Digital Map with Clean Renders:** For modern game aesthetics.
- **Blueprint Style with Glowing Neon Lines:** For a tactical "Architect's" view.
- **Watercolor and Ink Map:** For a melancholic sense of abandonment.
- **Cyber-Monolith Style:** Matte-black carbon fiber, chrome robotic textures, sharp cyber-lenses.
