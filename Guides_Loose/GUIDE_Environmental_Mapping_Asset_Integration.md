# GUIDE: Environmental Mapping and Asset Integration

## 1. GLOBAL WORLD RULES & THE PHYSICS OF PRESENCE

The environment must act as an autonomous antagonist. Power without biological or physical consequence is invalid geometry.

- **The Ruling Authority:**
    - **Tier 0 (Root Directory):** Immutable core, absolute gravity, mechanical torque.
    - **Tier 1 (Supreme Entities):** Authority Level 10.
- **Constraint Engineering:** Every high-voltage action has a measurable cost (Thermal Runaway, Proprioceptive Drift, Hydraulic Failure).
- **Somatic Supremacy:** Emotional states must be visually represented through thermodynamics, hydrodynamics, and muscular failure.
- **Resonance Cascades:** Electrostatic feedback occurs when multiple agents interact on the grid.

## 2. THE 20x20 GRID SYSTEM & INFRASTRUCTURE

The layout relies on a mathematical 20x20 matrix (400 m²) to control spatial flow.

- **Scale:** Origin (0,0) at Bottom-Left, Apex (20,20) at Top-Right. 1 cell = 1 m².
- **Primary Roads (X:9-10, Y:9-10):** 2m wide corridors for high-velocity transit and maximum exposure.
- **Tertiary Roads (X:5, 15; Y:5, 15):** 1m wide alleys for high-friction, claustrophobic movement.

## 3. ZONING ALGORITHMS & SPATIAL TENSION

**Spatial Tension Score = (Density Value * 3) + (Size Value * 1) + (Openness Value * 2)**

- **Central Hub ("The Kill Box") [8,8 to 12,12]:** Wide, Open, MAXIMUM tension.
- **Commercial Zones [6,6 to 14,14]:** Clustered around arteries for kinetic friction.
- **Industrial Zones [0,0 to 10,10; 10,10 to 20,20]:** Dense, Enclosed, HIGH tension.
- **Residential Zones [0,10 to 10,20; 10,0 to 20,10]:** Medium Density, Enclosed, SIS brakes (Tension Reset).
